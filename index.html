About SNAJE

SNAJE, founded on November 2nd, 2024 by Sarmad Zia and Antonio Juarez, is an innovative platform designed for students to network and share their progress in their academic careers. Our mission is to create a supportive community where students can connect, collaborate, and celebrate their educational achievements.

Connect with Peers

Network with students from various academic backgrounds

Find mentorship opportunities and learning materials

Display your skills and accomplishments
